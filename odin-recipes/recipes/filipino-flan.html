Filipino Flan

Description

Leche Flan is a dessert made-up of eggs and milk with a soft caramel on top. It resembles creme caramel and caramel custard.

Ingredients

  • 10 eggs (yolk only)
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 4 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 1 8-inch oval non-stick cake pan or llanera
  • 1 mixing bowl
  • 1 bamboo steamer
  • 1 pot
  • Tin Foil

Steps

  1. Separate the 10 egg yolk into a single mixing bowl
  2. Add 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract and 1 can of condensed milk into the bowl
  3. Mix the 3 ingredients together until the texture of the bowl is smooth
  4. Add 4 tablespoons of white sugar onto the non-stick pan/llanera
  5. Put the pan/llanera on stove and turn the heat on high
  6. Wait until the sugar caramelizes and place to the side to cool and harden
  7. While waiting to for the sugar to caramelize and cooldown, boil a pot of water
  8. Pour the mixed ingredents from earlier into the sugar caramelized pan/llanera
  9. Wrap the pan/llanera with tin foil and place it inside the bamboo steamer
  10. Place the bamboo steamer on top of the boiling pot and wait between approximately 28-30 minutes
  11. Once time has passed, safely pull the final product out of the steamer and cool inside a refrigerator for 2-3 hours.
  12. Use a knife and cut along the edges of the pan/llanera, flip, and serve
