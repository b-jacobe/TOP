Filipino Lumpia

Description

Lumpia or Lumpiang Shanghai is a type of Filipino egg roll. It's filled with ground pork along with minced onions, carrots, and seasonings.

Ingredients

  • 30-50 pieces lumpia wrapper
  • 3 cups cooking oil
  • 1 1/2 lbs ground pork
  • 2 pieces onion minced
  • 2 pieces carrots minced
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup parsley chopped
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Combine all filling ingredients in a bowl. Mix well
  2. Scoop around 1 to 1/2 tablespoons of filling and place over a piece of lumpia wrapper. Spread the filling and then fold both sides of the wrapper. Fold the bottom. Brush beaten egg mixture on top end of wrapper and roll up completely. Perform the same step until desired amount is achieved
  3. Heat oil in a cooking pot and deep fry lumpia in medium heat until it floats
  4. Remove from pot and let excess oil drip. Serve.
