New York Strip Steak

Description

New York Strip steak mixed with mushrooms and veggies is the ultimate combination for meat lovers.

Marinate with your favorite sauce to spice or liven the flavor up to highten the mouth savoring experience.

Ingredients

  • 8-10 oz N.Y. Strip Steak
  • 1/2 tablespoon of flavored oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 garlic cloves smashed
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 sprigs of fresh thyme
  • fresh mixed veggies
  • sliced mushrooms

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together your favorite marinade sauce to brush the steak during grilling and apply some on the raw steak. Turn to coat, cover and refrigerate for at least 2-3 hours.
  2. Preheat your frying pan or outdoor grill for high heat.
  3. Lightly brush the pan or grill with oil. Grill steak 5 minutes per side, or to desired doneness, brushing frequently with the reserved marinade mixture.
